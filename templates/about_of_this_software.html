Introduction

The Student Academic Portal is a modern web-based application designed to automate and enhance the management of student data, attendance, academic records, and institutional events. It streamlines the workflow of administrators, faculty, and students by offering a centralized platform for real-time academic operations.

Sap aims to reduce paperwork, increase data accuracy, and provide an intuitive interface for managing critical institutional activities from class attendance to event scheduling and student performance tracking.
