Core Features of SAP

1. Student Attendance Management

Faculty can mark attendance daily for each class with a simple and efficient interface. Attendance can be tracked monthly or semester-wise and is visually represented with color codes for easy analysis. Students can view their own attendance status in real-time, which improves transparency and accountability.

2. Student Marks Management

Teachers can enter internal exam scores, assignment marks, and lab evaluations. The system automatically calculates the percentage using predefined formulas. Students can view marks along with charts and comparisons using Chart.js, helping them understand their academic progress clearly.

3. College Timetable Scheduling

Admins can assign weekly timetables for each class, including subjects, periods, and teacher allocations. Faculty and students have access to personalized timetable views. The system prevents scheduling conflicts and ensures all slots are appropriately filled.

4. Academic Calendar & Events

A centralized academic calendar displays upcoming events, holidays, exam dates, and other important notifications. Admins can post new events and students can access them through a clean calendar interface. This keeps everyone informed and avoids miscommunication.

5. Student Feedback System

Students can submit feedback through a dedicated form that sends their message directly to the administrator's email. This creates a healthy communication channel where students can raise concerns, suggest improvements, or report technical issues.

6. Report Generation & Export

The system supports automatic PDF generation of marksheets and attendance records using jsPDF. These downloadable reports help students, faculty, and administrators to maintain documentation and share performance records when needed.
