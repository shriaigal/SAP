About AMS

The Student Academic Portal (SAP) is a comprehensive software solution designed to streamline and automate the process of recording and managing student attendance in colleges. This system is managed exclusively by college administrators, ensuring centralized control and security of attendance data. AMS simplifies attendance tracking by eliminating manual processes, reducing errors, and providing real-time insights into student attendance records.

Key Features:

  • Easy entry and updating of attendance records.
  • Secure storage and retrieval of attendance data.
  • Generation of attendance reports for students and faculty.
  • Notification system to alert students about attendance shortfalls.

With AMS, colleges can enhance operational efficiency, reduce manual errors, and improve communication between faculty and students.
